"From the simple kitchen cabinets, bathroom vanities or basement entertainment center to the most extravagant custom design, We can make it happen."

- Mike Kener, K4 Cabinetry Owner

K4 Cabinetry is a family run business that is based in West Haven, Utah. The founder and owner, Mike Kener has been a wood worker since 1992. Mike has always had a passion for building high quality cabinets and custom projects but was limited while working for other companies in Utah, so it was a natural move to create his own brand.

The Kener family of 4 finally established K4 Cabinetry in the early 2000's and has created some of the most amazing and beautiful cabinetry in the industry.All K4 Cabinets are created from the ground up in house by our talented team of craftsmen. This allows us to provide truly custom designs. We also have a team of in-house designers to help you find the perfect style for your needs. Our goal is to provide a quality experience tailored to fit your individual needs and goals. We want you to LOVE your cabinetry.

Meet our Designers!

K4 Cabinetry values our amazing designers. They are here to help you get the results you dream of!

Jacqueline Miller

I was born and raised in Utah and graduated with my Bachelor's Degree from Weber State University in 2005. I have always had a passion for design. I love being able to take an idea and make it a reality. Helping my clients bring their dreams to life is my goal and reward. At K4 Cabinetry our team is ready and excited to help you! We want you to love your cabinets and us!

Jeremy Garza

Jeremy Garza has been in the cabinet industry for over 10 years. He has designed in Houston and Dallas, Texas and is now here in the great state of Utah. He takes pride in seeing each job through from start to finish - from close discussion of aesthetic and practical considerations to drawing, production, and installation. From the smallest job to the largest, Jeremy will work with you to create the home of your dreams.
