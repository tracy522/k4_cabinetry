"From the simple kitchen cabinets, bathroom vanities or basement entertainment center to the most extravagant custom design, We can make it happen."

- Mike Kener, K4 Cabinetry Owner

K4 Cabinetry is a family run business that is based in West Haven, Utah. The founder and owner, Mike Kener has been a wood worker since 1992. Mike has always had a passion for building high quality cabinets and custom projects but was limited while working for other companies in Utah, so it was a natural move to create his own brand.

The Kener family of 4 finally established K4 Cabinetry in the early year of 2000 and has created some of the most amazing and beautiful cabinetry in the industry.
